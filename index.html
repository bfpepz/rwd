Responsive Web Design

In the year of our Lorde 2017, more than half of all traffic on the web is coming from mobile devices. What does that mean for web design? It must be responsive.

What does it mean to make a responsive site?

Responsive design is the purposeful construction of sites that will conform and optimize to a variety of displays and devices. It is composed of three key elements:

  • flexible, grid layout
  • flexible images and media
  • media queries

At the core of desinging a responsive site is relative sizing. Text, elements, media - relative units are key to creating a site that will display effectively on a desktop monitor, phone, tablet, or even the browser of a gaming console. Responsive design solves the problem of creating a cohesive user experience across all devices.
